All about Ollie!

Who is Ollie?

Ollie is Manav Khosla's dog. He is a cavalier king charles spaniel dog breed.
He was born on August 12 and he loves all people. He is currently 6 months
old and is considered a puppy.

What does Ollie like to do?

Since Ollie is a puppy he has lots of energy. This means he loves to play! Some
things we play with Ollie are fetch, tug of war and we do puppy training with him
with our doggy treats. We taught Ollie how to sit and stay. For example, if we were
to place a treat in front of Ollie and said stay, he would not move until we said eat.

What does Ollie require?

As fun as Ollie is he has lots of work. We have to take Ollie on walks every day for
at least 60 minutes. We also have to give him food during the day and finally we have
to take Ollie outside to go to the bathroom multiple times during the day.
